Why Advocate?

In short, because it works!

Habitat for Humanity was founded in 1976 by Millard and Linda Fuller.Today, Habitat for Humanity has built, rehabilitated and repaired homes for millions of people in thousands of communities in over 70 countries. Globally, we have helped more than 6.8 million people build strength, stability and independence through housing.

Advocating on behalf of those living in poor conditions is a priority for Habitat. We wholeheartedly believe that a decent place to live can remove barriers to opportunity, success and health that might have been part of a familyâ€™s life for years, if not generations.

To address that level of need, we invite you to advocate with us for housing policies and systems that address the global need for housing.

The core of our advocacy is not accepting the current policy's flaws, but rather trying to change it in a way that helps those in need of decent housing.

Our advocacy is part of a broader effort to improve public policies and institutions. Influencing public opinion and convincing leaders
to implement laws and practices that influence housing and human settlements are part of our strategy.

Habitat for Humanity opens its doors to individuals and groups who want to lend their time, skills, and hearts to help build homes, and advocate our mission. Together, we help transform lives, and give hope to families in need.
